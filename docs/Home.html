What makes a team a champion?

the answer is home

After searching through area income and population, we found that cities which offer higher pay and have a higher population produce more championship teams than those who don't. The leading city in North America is New York, NY, with a total of 61 championship wins between MLB, MLS, NBA, NFL, and NHL. Their population in 2018 was 8,390,081 with an average annual income of $76,565.
